About Us

Located on Manhattan's Lower East Side, CanCode Cat Cafe has an fun global menu, house-made desserts, fully stocked drink menu (including boba!) - and a big, furry family of adoptable cats. Our kitties roam freely around the sunlit catio area or our restaurant with plush seat for both cats and humans!

CanCode Cat Cafe's mission is to find loving homes for cats pulled from the city's “at-risk” list by our partner, Blueberry Cats Rescue. The at-risk list is the last stand for New York City's cats - they are scheduled to be euthanized shortly thereafter. At this point Blueberry Cat Rescue activates its network of foster homes and pulls as many cats as possible from the list. Some of these lucky kitties end up at CanCode Cat Cafe, where they're able to be adopted by our guests. We are humbled to be a part of this extraordinary rescue network. A portion of our proceeds goes back to Blueberry Cat Rescue to help their efforts in saving at risk kitties!
